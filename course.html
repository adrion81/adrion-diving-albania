Courses We Offer 🤿

Becoming a scuba diver is the first step in a journey that will forever change the way you experience the world. You will explore the wonders of a weightless underwater world, interact with incredible marine life and discover ecosystems hidden beneath the surface. Learning to dive is much easier than you think and the best way to experience this amazing new sport is the CMAS Try Scuba program. The best part, your materials are FREE! The digital materials will help prepare you for this first taste of a great underwater experience. If you want to dive anywhere you want, with anyone you want, the Open Water Diver program is your international certification to be part of an exclusive group of underwater explorers that most people only dream about joining.

Scuba Diver

Course price: 220,00E Entry level diving licence. Designed for those not wishing to spend 2 days getting their scuba diver and for those who dive only whilst on holiday. After the 2 day course the student will have completed the first 3 modules of the open water course. This licence will allow the student to go to a depth of 12 metres and to be able to purchase their own equipment. Restrictions: Minimum entry age-14 years. Once completed the student will be able to go diving with either an instructor or divemaster only. Is accredited towards to be fall Open Water licence.

Open Water

Course price: 350,00E The Open Water Diver course is an introduction into the world of scuba diving. After the 3 day course, which consists of: 2 dives a day (and) Theory (both instructor and video), a student will be able to explore the underwater world in a relaxing, enjoyable and undemanding environment to a depth of 18 metres.

Advanced Open Water

Course price: 300,00E The Advanced Open Water is designed as an extension of the Open Water Diver course. It is designed for the student to gain additional knowledge, which include greater depths and underwater navigation. Take the next step! With the Advanced Open Water Diver Course, you will expand the scuba skills and won’t need to worry about the limited depth. You can explore new places and mysterious spots that required drift, deep or night diving experiences. You can start right after completing the Open Water Diver course. Night dive Wreck Dive Cave Dive Reef Dive Deep Dive Wall Dive Volcano Dive

Divemaster

Course price:650,00E Divemaster is the highest level in Scuba Diving before reaching the Instructor levels. It is regarded as being the first step into the CMAS Leadership System. This program will help shape your abilities and attitde toward becoming a professional level diving educator.
